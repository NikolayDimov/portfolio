About me

Greetings! I'm a Software Developer based in Bulgaria, with a strong background in technology, product management, and business strategy.

For the past nine years, I've been part of Yara Europe, advancing through roles from Technical Support Specialist to Product Manager, and now Software Developer. This journey has deepened my expertise in agro and meteorological software solutions. I develop software that directly addresses industry challenges, bridging technology with practical solutions.

As a Full-Stack Developer, I specialize in JavaScript, React, React Native, Angular, Express, REST and GraphQL, with expertise in building scalable and high-performance applications using tools like NestJS, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Docker, AWS, and Styled Components. I leverage Git and Jira for collaboration, and I am dedicated to software quality through testing with Jest, Mocha, Chai, and Postman. With a Master’s degree in Economics and a Software Development certification from SoftUni, I bring a unique perspective that combines technical innovation with strategic business insights.

I’m passionate about developing solutions that not only meet technical requirements but also drive efficiency and create real impact.
